Your First GRC Lead Left

Reading reference T1: The Inversion Thesis Approved

This reading helps learners see how inherited instincts keep operating after the person who introduced them leaves. Use it to teach legacy-vs-operating primitives: which rituals still serve the programme, which only preserve old assumptions, and where reality needs to be re-observed. It is a useful reference for sessions about inherited controls, stale policies, and programme archaeology.
