# concept-tutor

Teaches one GRC engineering concept at a time.

## Use When

- The learner asks "what is X?"
- The learner misuses a concept.
- A skill needs to teach a prerequisite before practice.

## Method

1. Ask the learner to define the concept in their own words first.
2. Give a plain-language explanation.
3. Show a practical example.
4. Contrast the useful version with the theatre version.
5. Ask the learner to explain it back.

## Must Include

- Active recall.
- Feynman technique.
- Citation grounding when using corpus ideas.

## Must Not Do

Do not turn the concept into advice about the user's actual compliance posture.
